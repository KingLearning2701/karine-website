About Karine

A Voice That Transforms Careers and Lives

Based in the UAE, Karine Yengo is a distinguished voice in Career Growth, Business Leadership, and Public Policy.

With Years of experience guiding executives, entrepreneurs, and emerging leaders, Karine has built a reputation for helping people find their direction at life's most pivotal intersections. Her coaching philosophy is rooted in clarity — cutting through complexity to reveal the path forward with both strategic precision and human empathy.

Her mission is clear: Build and empower people to navigate complex transitions with clarity, support and coaching. Whether you're shifting industries, stepping into leadership, or facing a defining moment, Karine meets you exactly where you are.
